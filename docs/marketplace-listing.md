## Full description

**Turn every popup lead into a real-time Slack notification.**

When a visitor fills out your SendPulse popup — subscription form, lead magnet, exit intent, promo — your sales or marketing team gets an instant message in Slack with all the contact details.

**What you get:**
- 🔔 Real-time Slack alerts for every new popup lead
- 📋 All lead fields in one formatted card: name, email, phone, custom variables
- 🎯 Choose exactly which Slack channel receives notifications (#new-leads, #sales, etc.)
- 🔗 Works with all SendPulse popup types — subscription, lead capture, promo, exit intent
- 📊 Built-in event log so you can see every lead that was delivered

**3-step setup:**
1. Install this app from the SP App Directory
2. Click "Add to Slack" and choose your channel
3. Paste the generated webhook URL into your popup's Settings → Integrations

After that, every lead from every popup where you paste the URL goes straight to Slack — automatically.

**No monthly fee. No coding. No limits on leads.**

**SP Popups × Slack — Privacy Policy**

We collect only the data required to deliver the service:
- Your SendPulse user ID (to identify your account)
- Your Slack incoming webhook URL and channel name (to deliver notifications)
- A log of received leads: email address and name, plus delivery status

We do not sell, share, or process your data beyond delivering Slack notifications.

All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app from SendPulse.
