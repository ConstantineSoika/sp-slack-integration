Privacy Policy

What we collect

We collect only the data required to deliver the service:

  • Your SendPulse user ID (to identify your account)

What we don't do

We do not sell, share, or process your data beyond delivering Slack notifications. We do not use cookies beyond a session cookie required for authentication.

All data is permanently and automatically deleted the moment you uninstall the app from SendPulse.
